Find out more about Ultraleaps haptics hardware and software.

To add mid-air haptics, youll need either a STRATOS Explore or STRATOS Inspire haptic module. Both come with a Leap Motion Controller included, so this single unit is all you need for both hand tracking and haptics. They also require the Ultraleap Haptics SDK to run.
