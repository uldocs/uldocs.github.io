What you'll need
****************

To begin using our advanced hand tracking in your VR or AR application simply attach a
`Leap Motion Controller <https://www.ultraleap.com/product/leap-motion-controller/>`_ or
`Stereo IR 170 Camera Module <https://www.ultraleap.com/product/stereo-ir-170/>`_ to the headset and
`download our latest Hand Tracking Software release <https://leap2.ultraleap.com/gemini-downloads/>`_.
