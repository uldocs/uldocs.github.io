*********
Ultraleap Plugin Features
*********

The Ultraleap Unreal Plugin comes with a collection of features to help you to get started with hand tracking in your application.

The majority of our examples are tailored towards XR, but most of the features translate to Tabletop with some small modifications.

.. warning:: This page will link you to the XR section of our documentation and may discuss unnecessary steps for use with Tabletop configurations
